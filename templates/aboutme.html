A little bit about me

Hello! As you saw in my pervious page my name is Naiara Munich. I am the daughter of two Argentinian immigrants and speak fluent spanish. I am originally from Los Angeles, California but graduated from La Canada High school in 2019 and am now grateful to be part of Barnard class of 2023!

I intend to follow the Barnard chemistry track and hopefully apply for the 4+1 masters degree track in chemical engineering Barnard has with SEAS. I am part of the fourth cohort of the Sciene Pathwyas Scholar Program, also known as sp^2, a program that funds minority students summer research in NYC. This summer I was going to research lithium batteries in Lauren Marbella's lab hosted at

SEAS' Electrochemical Energy Center. Sadly, due to the current cicumstance, I will not be partaking in summer research this year, but I am looking forward to next summer :)

In high school I was an avid potter and reader. Not wanting to lose my touch with ceramics I joined the Barnard Clay Collective my second semester and enjoyed my time working with clay - albeit for a short time (thanks COVID). I am a huge fantasy nerd and enjoy books such asThe Mortal Instruments, Throne of Glass, and The Nevernight Chronicles.
