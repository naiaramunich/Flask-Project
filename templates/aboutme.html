Hello! As you saw in my pervious page my name is Naiara Munich. I am Argentinian and speak fluent spanish. I am originally from Los Angeles, California but now study chemistry at Barnard College. I graduated from La Canada High school in 2019 and am grateful to be part of Barnard class of 2023! I intend to follow the Barnard chemistry track and hopefully apply for the 4+1 in chemical engineering Barnard has with SEAS. I am part of the fourth cohort of the Sciene Pathwyas Scholar Program (sp2) which funds our summer research in NYC.

In high school I was an avid potter and reader. Not wanting to lose my touch with ceramics I joined the Barnard Clay Collective second semester and enjoyed my time working with clay - albeit for a short time (thanks COVID). I am a huge fantasy nerd and enjoy books such asThe Mortal Instruments, Throne of Glass, and The Nevernight Chronicles.
