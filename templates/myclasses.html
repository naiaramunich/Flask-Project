Favorite Classes

My two favorite classes taken at Columbia so far have been Cine y Censura en la Espana de Franco (which I took first semester) and Engi1006, both for vastly different reasons.

I have taken spanish classes all my life, however, Cine y Censura brought a new approach to the study of the spanish language, which was refreshing after many years of strictly studying grammar and vocabulary.

I also chose ENGI1006 as one of my favorite classes becayse it was the first coding class where I actually learned how to code. While I did struggle with the homeworks, finding the errors in my code and making the system run succesfully was incredibly rewarding.
